A02. Artificial Intelligence Techniques, Applications and Ethics

The documentary AlphaGo: The Movie follows the creation of DeepMind’s AI as it masters the ancient game of Go, using neural networks and deep learning, culminating in its historic 2016 match against world champion Lee Sedol in South Korea tournament.

Beyond competition, the film explores how AlphaGo learns through self-play, changing perceptions of artificial intelligence and raising ethical questions about fairness, transparency, and human creativity, showing AI as a powerful learning system rather than a simple tool for society today.

Read the following text about AlphaGo and its relevance in the field of artificial intelligence carefully in order to prepare your answers for a series of questions that will be presented in upcoming activities.
